How to Apply

Applicants are requested to fill out the admission form attached in the FPM Section and attach all relevant supporting documents.

Admission Eligibility of Students

A. Master’s Degree or equivalent in Engineering and Technology/ Management/ Economics/ Social Science/ Biological Science/ Pure Science/ Commerce/ Humanities with FIRST CLASS shall be considered for admission to Fellow Program.

B. Those appearing for their final examination in the respective discipline can also apply. Such students if selected shall be provisionally admitted provided they complete all requirements in obtaining their Master’s Degree before 30th September of the year of admission. The admission of these candidates shall remain provisional until they produce the mark sheet proving that they satisfy the eligibility criteria. The deadline for submitting the final year mark sheet is 31 December 2024.

C. Application Procedure
The application shall be made in the prescribed form available with the Institution. Attested copies of all the necessary Certificates and testimonials are to be attached with the applications. The candidate must submit an abstract of about 5000 words on the area of research interest (tentative research proposal) along with his/ her application.

D. Selection Criteria
Selection for the Fellow Program in the Institutions approved by AICTE for the Fellow Program shall be on the basis of the following criteria:
1. Academic Qualification & Work Experience.
2. Research Proposal.
3. Personal Interview.
