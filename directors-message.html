DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

Dr. Nandita Mishra
Director

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you all to Chetana’s Institute of Management & Research. With a vision to develop CIMR, as a niche business school nurturing professional excellence, we are committed to prepare students as successful & ethical business leaders to meet the current and future needs of the industry.

It has always been our endeavour to strengthen curricular and co-curricular activities and ensure high quality learning for our students. Over the last twenty-five years, Chetana’s Institute of Management & Research has covered several milestones in the field of management education. We believe in constantly engaging in dialogue with the corporate world to understand the current trends and accordingly design our Programmes to skill the budding managers.

Academic Programmes at CIMR are designed to cater to the increasingly complex skills required in today's globalised world. It has always been the endeavour of the Institute to strengthen the faculty team. We have a good mix of dynamic, seasoned academics and industry experienced faculty members, who work towards institution building and student mentoring.

Distinguished speakers and guest faculty from academics, industry, business, government, and other sectors like sustainability development career planning, culture and international relations frequently visit us and interact with the students.

The Institute inspires its students to strive for thought leadership through several specialized areas and through the Personality Development Programme, in the form of corporate events and extracurricular activities. The Management Development Programme Division and Consulting contributes more effectively to the professional growth of managers and administrators.

In the era of globalisation, all - round exposure is mandatory for all. It is important for students to appreciate cultural differences and gain insights in academics and market driven value-added courses. The perfect blend of academics and beyond-classroom learning prepares our student towards industry readiness and develops socially responsible managers and entrepreneurs to meet the challenges of the business environment.

We invite everybody to learn, grow, collaborate and excel with us.
